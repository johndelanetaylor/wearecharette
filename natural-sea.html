Natural Sea

The Reel Story

This is the beginning of a beautiful friendship: One fateful day, Emily was pushing her daughter Eleanor (then about 18 months) in a swing at the park, and struck up a conversation with a lovely-looking mom pushing a similarly-aged baby girl nearby. They mostly chatted about childrearing and things to do in St. Augustine (as the other mom was visiting on vacation from New England) but at the very end of the conversation, Emily mentioned that she was a graphic designer. The other mom then introduced Emily to her husband, who works in marketing at Blue Marble Brands, and the rest is history. Six months later, we were starting work on Natural Sea.
